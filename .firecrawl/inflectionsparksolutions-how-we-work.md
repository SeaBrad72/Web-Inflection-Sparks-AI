## Our Engagement Flow

Five modular stages, built to match your readiness and goals.

Most ISS projects follow a five-stage journey:

1. Discovery — Align on context, risks, and opportunity

2. Strategy — Build a roadmap and operating model with buy-in

3. Execution — Deliver fast, measure progress, stay agile

4. Scale — Extend patterns, enable teams, build resilience

5. Transition — Codify, hand off, and prepare for autonomy


These phases reflect how we structure engagements across product, tech, and leadership transformation.

Every phase is modular. Some clients engage for two weeks. Others partner for 12 months. Our role is to meet you where you are—and leave you stronger than we found you.
