# About   Inflection Spark Solutions

## Empowering organizations through strategic alignment, leadership, and execution

## Who We Are

At Inflection Spark Solutions, our team brings decades of experience building global teams, launching transformative platforms, and navigating major technological shifts—from the early days of the Internet to today's agentic AI revolution. Our expertise spans diverse industries including mobility, media and entertainment, fintech, healthcare, SaaS, and telecommunications. We believe technology itself does not transform businesses—aligned people, clear strategy, and committed leadership do.

## Our Leadership

Inflection Spark Solutions is led by Bradley P. James, whose extensive experience includes executive leadership roles and transformative technology initiatives at global companies such as BMW, Sony, Technicolor, Deluxe, Qwest Communications, and InGrooves/UMG.

Bradley’s career has consistently focused on aligning technology strategy, organizational culture, and leadership to drive meaningful business outcomes.
